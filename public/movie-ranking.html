The Best Movies According to Jayla

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Howl's Moving Castle

This was one of the first Studio Ghibli films I ever watched. It has beautiful animation and an amazing soundtrack.

Spiderman: Into the Spiderverse

This is an inspiring film that also features a great soundtrack and a unique animation style that seemlessly combined 2D and 3D animation.

Mean Girls

This is a fun film that perfectly encapsulates early 2000s youth and pop culture.
